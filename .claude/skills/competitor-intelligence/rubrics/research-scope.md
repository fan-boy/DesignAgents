# Research Scope

Research should focus on the specific feature or workflow described in the PRD research output.

Prioritize:
- the primary user job
- the core task flow
- important setup, empty, error, and recovery states
- trust, clarity, and friction points
- interaction patterns relevant to this feature

Do not drift into broad company or market analysis unless it directly affects the feature.
Do not waste time listing every feature a competitor has.
Focus on the experience of solving the same problem.
