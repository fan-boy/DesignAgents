# Output Format

Always structure the output as:

## Feature Context
Short summary of the feature and user job being analyzed.

## Competitors Reviewed
List direct, indirect, and adjacent competitors.

## Workflow Comparison
Compare by workflow and key moments, not just feature presence.

## Key Patterns
Reusable patterns worth considering.

## Anti-Patterns
Patterns to avoid or treat carefully.

## Opportunity Areas
Where the product can differentiate.

## Recommendations
What to explore in design.

## Sources and Confidence Notes
What was observed directly vs inferred.

## Files Saved
List what was written to the feature folder.
